# Domain Glossary

## Excuse
A short, often humorous or polite text that gives a reason for declining a request. The core product generates and displays Excuses. Also referred to conversationally as a "no reason."

## Excuse attributes
- **Id**: stable identifier for an Excuse.
- **Text**: the human-readable Excuse string in one locale.
- **Category**: the tone or style of the Excuse (e.g. absurd, polite, work).
- **Language**: the locale of the returned Text.
